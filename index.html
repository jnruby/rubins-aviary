The birds of Des canyons aux étoiles…

Olivier Messiaen notated birdsong from four continents into his 1974 score. Each entry pairs the name as it appears with the species behind it, and a field recording.

Compiled from the score of Des canyons aux étoiles… (1971–74). Recording links point to the Macaulay Library, All About Birds, YouTube, and other third-party archives.
